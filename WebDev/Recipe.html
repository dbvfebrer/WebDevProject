How recipes are made

Sometimes you get confused when there are so many elements in front of you that you no longer know what to do - the possibilities are endless. Sometimes, though, there is too little to cook with and too limited options of what can be done. So, the best approach is to break down the elements of a recipe or dish to help you isolate ingredient categories to make it easier to think of what to cook.
